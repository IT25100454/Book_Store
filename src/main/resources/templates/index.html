Reading culture

Habits, collecting, and a life with books.

Reading habits

A lasting habit usually begins small: a chapter before the day accelerates, a page after dinner, a pencil beside the bed. The best routine is the one that makes attention easy to repeat.

Benefits of reading

Books sharpen language, train focus, and create emotional range. They make complicated lives legible by letting us rehearse other minds, other histories, and other futures.

Book collecting

A collection is a portrait built slowly. Keep the books that changed your pace, the editions you return to, and the volumes that make a room feel intellectually alive.
